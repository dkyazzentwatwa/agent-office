# Reporting Folder Intro

## Purpose
Use this folder for summaries, dashboards, status reports, and decision-ready updates.

## What goes here
- Weekly or monthly reports
- Office summaries
- KPI snapshots
- Leadership-ready updates

## How to use this folder
Write reports here when the goal is visibility, not execution. Keep outputs concise, current, and tied to real metrics or observed status.

## Notes for AI agents
Use this folder when asked for summaries, rollups, or executive-ready reporting. Prefer evidence and trends over vague narrative.
