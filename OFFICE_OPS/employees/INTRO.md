# Employees Folder Intro

## Purpose
Use this folder as the source of truth for who is on the office team and how capacity is distributed.

## What goes here
- Employee roster records
- Utilization and availability notes
- Role coverage summaries

## How to use this folder
Start here when deciding who should own a task. Check current capacity before assigning new work.

## Notes for AI agents
Use this folder to understand team structure, workload, and escalation routing. Do not assign work purely by availability if the role fit is wrong.
